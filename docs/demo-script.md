# FinHub demo video script

**Target length**: 90 seconds. No voiceover required — clean screen recording with on-screen captions works too.

**Recording setup**: 1280×800 browser at 100% zoom. Dark mode (default). Pre-seed an account with one portfolio + a few holdings (AAPL, NVDA, JPM) and one watchlist item (TSLA). Apply migrations 0001–0006 first.

---

## Scene 1 — Landing (0:00 → 0:08)

- Open `/`
- Caption: "FinHub — your financial command center."
- Hover the "Get started" button briefly, then click "Explore dashboard."

## Scene 2 — Dashboard (0:08 → 0:18)

- Land on `/dashboard`. Show market cards animating in.
- Caption: "Markets, portfolio, news — one view."
- Hover the S&P 500 card so the sparkline stands out.

## Scene 3 — Screener (0:18 → 0:32)

- Click `Screener` in the sidebar.
- Caption: "Filter the market in real time."
- Toggle the **Mega** cap pill.
- Open the **Sector** dropdown, select Technology.
- Type `2` into Dividend Yield min.
- Click "Save screen" → name it "Mega-cap dividend tech" → Save.

## Scene 4 — Ticker deep-dive (0:32 → 0:50)

- Click on `NVDA` in the table.
- Caption: "AI-generated buy/sell signal."
- Wait for the AI gauge to animate; pan to the Bull / Bear / Risks columns.
- Scroll through Financial Highlights and the AI-summarized News list.

## Scene 5 — AI Portfolio Builder (0:50 → 1:10)

- Click `Builder` in the sidebar.
- Caption: "Answer 7 questions, get a tailored allocation."
- Speed-run steps 1 → 7 (Goal: Retirement, Horizon: 10+, Risk: 4, Initial: $25,000, Monthly: $1,000, Preferences: Growth + International, Experience: Intermediate).
- Click "Generate recommendation" — let the loading spinner show briefly.
- Show the allocation pie + ETF cards animating in.
- Click "Save to Portfolio" → green success toast appears.

## Scene 6 — Portfolio Tracker (1:10 → 1:22)

- Click `Portfolio` in the sidebar.
- Caption: "Live P/L, sector breakdown, performance history."
- Show the new portfolio with all the AI-recommended holdings.
- Toggle the performance chart from 1M → 1Y → All.

## Scene 7 — Calculators + close (1:22 → 1:30)

- Quick cuts: `/calculators/dividend` (drag the years input from 10 → 30, watch the numbers recompute), `/calculators/position-size` (entry 100 → stop 95 → target 115, R/R bar animates).
- End on the FinHub logo with caption: **"Free forever. Built for taste-driven investors."**

## Music

Mid-tempo lo-fi or minimal electronic. Avoid anything with abrupt drops — the wizard step transitions look better with a steady beat.

## Capture tips

- Use Chrome with extensions disabled and a clean profile to avoid popups.
- Record at 60fps if possible (the Framer Motion transitions look 2× better).
- Trim mouse-search dead time aggressively — 90s leaves no room for hesitation.
- Export at H.264 1080p, 8-12 Mbps for landing-page playback.
